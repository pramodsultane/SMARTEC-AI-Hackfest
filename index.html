What makes a winning idea?

  • Clear problem statement — Describe the pain point and its business impact
  • AI-first approach — Explain how AI specifically solves or accelerates this
  • Feasibility — Show it's achievable with current technology within 3-6 months
  • Measurable impact — Define KPIs: time saved, cost reduced, quality improved
  • Category fit — Map it to AI for SDLC, AI for Ops, or AI for Business
